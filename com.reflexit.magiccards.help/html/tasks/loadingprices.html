Loading sellers prices

Seller's Price is a price of cards of a card for sale by a given provider. Currently supported providers are "MTG Fanatic" and "Find Magic Cards". If you have preference between these you can go to Window-Preferences->Magic Preferences and select one in the "Card Prices Provider".

Here is how to laod and view the prices:

  • Step 1: Click on the preferences button which is the spreadsheet looking button that is second from the left in the line of buttons in the upper right hand corner.
  • Step 2: Once that is open, scroll down and add a check to the seller price box. After that click apply and then OK.
  • Step 3: In the same upper right hand corner of links, you'll see an upside down pyramid (icon is third from the right next to the minimize and maximize button.), click it.
  • Step 4: In the list that will appear, click "Load Extra Fields" which is the 5th link from the top.
  • Step 5: Add a check to the "Price" field and click OK. This will update the cards and adding the price to the "Seller Price" field.
